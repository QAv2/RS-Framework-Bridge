## Books referenced

The site lists [`Collected Writings of K.V.K. Nehru`](https://reciprocalsystem.org/books) (1982) — a 5-book set per the search result, edited by KVK Nehru and Rainer Huck. Print only. Not in the PDF archive.

Nehru also references his own work in *Reciprocity* via these standard footnotes:
- *Nothing But Motion* (Larson 1979) — primary Larson reference for atomic structure, mass components, inter-regional ratio (this is the book the Nehru/Peret papers cite most heavily).
- *Basic Properties of Matter* (Larson 1988) — primary reference for inter-atomic distances, photon as rotational space, scalar motion theory.
- *New Light on Space and Time* (Larson 1965) — primary reference for inter-regional transmission factors (1/c, 1/c²).
- *Universe of Motion* (Larson 1984) — astronomical phenomena.
- *The Neglected Facts of Science* (Larson 1982) — scalar dimensions.
- *The Case Against the Nuclear Atom* (Larson 1963) — the foundational claim that the atom has no internal parts and no nuclear force exists, which Nehru extends.

Larson's books are not in scope for *this* corpus — they are upstream of Nehru/Peret and Joe already has them indirectly via the RS2-101..109 distillations and the Peret EE-dictionary.
