# Reciprocity Journal — Index of Mass / Nuclear Content

The full Master Index PDF (Peret) is in `_pdfs/periodicals-master-index.pdf` and `_text/periodicals-master-index.txt` (2682 lines, 1.3 MB). It indexes:
- *Reciprocity* (the quarterly ISUS journal, 1971–~2010)
- *ISUS News* (member newsletter)
- *New Science Advocates* (early newsletter, 1971)

Total inventory: ~30+ years of issues, hundreds of papers.

This file lists ONLY the entries from the master index relevant to the **Yang-Mills mass-gap** problem (mass derivation, nuclear cohesion, hadron spectrum, atomic-zone partition, MeV calibration). For complete inventory see the master index PDF.

---

## Citation format

`Series.Volume.Issue-Page` where:
- A = 1971–~1976 (volumes I–X)
- B = ~1977–~1983 (volumes XI–XIV)
- C = 1984–~1991 (volumes XV–XX)
- D = 1991–1996 (volumes XXI–XXV)
- E = 1997+ (volume XXVI on)

So `B 11.1-8` = Series B, Volume 11, № 1, page 8 = Reciprocity XI(1).

## What's accessible online

All of the **Series B–E** Nehru papers above (and most Peret papers from D onwards) appear on `reciprocalsystem.org` as PDFs. The **Series A** content (1971–1976) is only partially available — many of the early Larson, Meyer, Cramer pieces aren't in the modern PDF archive but appear in the Master Index.

The **two `Sub-atomic Mass Recalculated Update` papers** at D 25.1-8 and D 25.2-25 are referenced separately from the original D 24.2-13. These updates may contain additional refinement of Peret's mass-component recalculation; they're not currently in this corpus and would be a good next-pull if Joe's Yang-Mills derivation needs more decimal-place fidelity on the proton/neutron/hydrogen mass values.
